Button Sherlock


Share to vote for your favourite ideas!

In Button Sherlock you can press any button that's sticking up, and you win when all the buttons are down! Simple? Maybe not, because each button is linked to other buttons, and pressing a button will flip all the buttons it's connected to. Can you figure out how to get all pegs down without going around in circles?
