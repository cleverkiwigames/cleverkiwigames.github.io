Button Sherlock

Deductive puzzle game

In Button Sherlock you can press any button that's sticking up, and you win when all buttons are down! Simple? Maybe not, because each button is secretly linked to other buttons, and pressing a button will flip all the buttons its connected to. Can you deduce what buttons are connected and then use that information to work out the sequence to press them in?
