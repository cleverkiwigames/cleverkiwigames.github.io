Other names:

  • Button Deduction
  • Peg Detective
  • Button Bop

There are only rules in Button Deduction: you can tap a button that is up, and you win when all buttons are down. However, all the buttons are secretly linked, and when you tap a button all the buttons connected to it flip!

Planned features:

  • Story mode
  • Various level themes, such as Autumn and Winter
  • Intuitive level designer
  • Infinite mode
  • Hints
  • Advanced gameplay, such as:
    • One-way links
    • Buttons that you can only press once
    • Buttons that you must press at least once
