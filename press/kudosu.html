Description

Kudosu

A Deceptively Simple Number Puzzle Game

Kudosu is a number puzzle game with simple rules and a lot of depth. Relying on your basic instincts can have dire ramifications in Kudosu, as the obvious move is often ill-advised. An ability to deduce patterns and see into the future are highly recommended.

The influence of Sudoku in the creation of Kudosu becomes apparent when one finds out that the rules fit in a single tweet. However, these simple rules hide a depth that can only be appreciated through experience. You will begin to see patterns. They will often be incorrect. You may notice similarities between levels. They're actually very different.

Each possible level, varying in grid size and difficulty, were automatically generated by computer. Multitudes of levels were then analyzed by hand, through a process that weighed novelty, engagement and challenge, and 50 were chosen to represent the spirit of Kudosu in the final game. The myriad of other combinations can still be discovered though - after completing every featured level, a bonus Infinite Mode will be unlocked, which generates new Kudosu levels indefinitely. Be warned though, some levels you will encounter in this mode can be much more difficult than any you've seen before...

History

Clever Kiwi Games is constantly trying out new ideas and innovating in the space of games that hope to appeal to the oft-overlooked demographics of older gamers and female gamers. After the infinite runner TRACE: Duplex, puzzles were chosen to be the genre of the successor. A desire to create a simple number game began a creative process that ended in the development of Kudosu, with such simple rules that they rival Sudoku in brevity.

The design of Kudosu is largely thanks to Felix Braden, who has distributed a number of his high-quality fonts for free. All of the icons in the game are derived from his Orchidee font, and the paper-based nature of the game (with paper textures provided by transparenttextures.com) was inspired by the oriental feel of that typeface.

About Clever Kiwi Games

Clever Kiwi Games is an Irish independent game studio that tries to appeal to a mass casual audience by creating small games with cunning mechanics that provide a lot of replay value. We don't just want to provide our players with convenient time sinks, but also mental gyms and outlets for their creativity. In this way we hope to create games that also appeal to older gamers and female gamers, demographics that (while as prevalent as others) are often neglected when it comes to choice with modern mobile gaming. The name Clever Kiwi Games itself was chosen with those overlooked demographics in mind, and tries to convey the idea of simple, small games for people that like to flex their mind muscles while they have fun.
