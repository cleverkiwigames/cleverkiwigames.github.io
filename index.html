Kudosu

A deceptively simple number puzzle game


Kudosu is a number puzzle game with three simple rules: 1. tap a tile to reduce tiles on the same row and column by one, 2. tiles reduced to zero are cleared, and 3. clear all tiles to win! However, the caveat is that you can't tap tiles on the same row or column as a cleared tile... While these rules are brief, they hide a depth that can only be appreciated through experience - prepare to have your intuition challenged and your foresight stretched!

The design of Kudosu is largely thanks to Felix Braden, who has distributed a number of his high-quality fonts for free. All of the icons in the game are derived from his Orchidee font, and the paper-based nature of the game (with paper textures provided by transparenttextures.com) was inspired by the oriental feel of that typeface.

TRACE: Duplex

TRACE: Duplex is the debut game by Clever Kiwi Games. It's a modern infinite runner that tests both sides of your brain by challenging you to weave in and out of two parallel tracks, extending through a vibrant colourscape.

Created with a clean, modern design and intuitive gameplay, TRACE: Duplex is perfect for relaxing and taking your mind off things. A definitive casual game with great pick-up-and-play appeal, it's sure to help you pass time pleasantly.

About

Clever Kiwi Games was created with the idea of making fun casual games that appeal to everyone, regardless of age or gender. We try to make small games but with interesting gameplay that provides a lot of replay value, such as puzzles and arcade games. A lot of effort is also put into making these games as beautiful as possible so that each game is a unique experience.

Social is one of our passions at Clever Kiwi Games. We encourage gamers to engage with each other through online leaderboards, as well as through social media integration. We also love for people to engage with us personally - we relish feedback, and many of the updates you see in the newest versions of TRACE: Duplex were based on suggestions made by our followers. You can hit us up on our Facebook page and on our twitter @CleverKiwiGames. We're pretty active on those pages ourselves so check us out from time to time to get our latest updates.
