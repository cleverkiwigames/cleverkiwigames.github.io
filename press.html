Description

Clever Kiwi Games is an Irish independent game studio.

We try to appeal to a mass casual audience by creating small games with cunning mechanics that provide a lot of replay value. We don't just want to provide our players with convenient time sinks, but also mental gyms and outlets for their creativity. In this way we hope to create games that also appeal to older gamers and female gamers, demographics that (while as prevalent as others) are often neglected when it comes to choice with modern mobile gaming. The name Clever Kiwi Games itself was chosen with those overlooked demographics in mind, and tries to convey the idea of simple, small games for people that like to flex their mind muscles while they have fun.

History

Early history

Clever Kiwi Games was founded in 2016 by Sean Kelleher, after leaving his job in early 2015. While working on a mobile social application, he took a break for a weekend by creating a game from scratch in Javascript, his first in almost a decade. A simple puzzle game with plain graphics, and though never released, the game had nonetheless ignited a spark that wouldn't let him stop coming up with ideas for games.
